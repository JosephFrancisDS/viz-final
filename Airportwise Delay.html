Major Airports that causes the most delay in USA

Airports, admittedly, are largely at the mercy of air traffic control and weather when it comes to delays. This dashboard is another map plot of the airports to discover more information on them. I analyzed data from the Bureau of Transportation Statistics for 29 major U.S. airports to highlight which have experienced the least and most delayed departures over six years of period.

Key Findings:

  • We see if we take all the years and all the carriers, the airport which caused the most delay is Chicago O'Hare airport followed by Atlanta and Texas.
  • We can also see for different carriers, which airports caused the most delay e.g. When the carrier is Virgin America, we see that San Francisco and Los Angeles caused the most delay.
  • We can also hover our mouse on the bottom plot to see the exact geolocation of that particular airport.
